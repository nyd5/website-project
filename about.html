About Us

Connecting with other players in multiplayer games can be hard, especially in competitive games like League of Legends or Overwatch. We want to change that by flipping the formula on its head - instead of focusing on the quantity of matches by cramming players into sessions using limited metrics, we're instead focusing on the quality of matches by connecting players on a vastly greater number of metrics, so that when two players come together to play, they know that they'll enjoy the game with their new ally.
