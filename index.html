Lonely People

Even without COVID, the United States is already suffering from a loneliness epidemic. According to data from the OECD, Cigna, YouGov, and The Kaiser Family Foundation:
  • 60% of respondents reported often feeling lonely or isolated
  • 53% reported difficulty making new friends
  • 21% reported having no close friends at all

Fuel to the Fire

Now, with the pandemic, the problem has only gotten worse:
  • 31% of respondents reported feeling more lonely
  • Of those:
    • 53% reported difficulty making new friends
    • 21% reported having no close friends at all

Silver Lining

Luckily, the video games industry has received a major boost:
  • Overall sales saw a 35% year-over-year increase
  • Player engagement is up 130%
  • Twitch viewership increased by 50%
